My name is Mike Lawson. I've worked in Marketing for about 10 years at this point, and I've decided to make a career change. I've always enjoyed the analytical, data, and problem-solving aspects of my work more than I've enjoyed driving marketing strategy. So I'm learning to code.

I first learned HTML when I was 14 and didn't do much with it except make terrible Geocities pages with my friends. When I started working in email marketing, I picked HTML back up and learned some basic CSS to style emails.

Now I'm taking that knowledge and pushing it forward. I'm learning to actually code so I can make really cool stuff on the internet!

In my personal life, I'm a baker, a woodworker, and a knitter. I just love making new things, and coding is another extension of that.
